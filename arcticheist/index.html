are you ready for the ultimate heist?

You and your team of hackers will attempt to infiltrate the secret Arctic lair of one of the world's wealthiest crime lords. Are you skilled enough to make off with his treasure? The Arctic Heist contest will take place in 3 rounds, each concerning a different aspect of web programming. Each round will be approximately 40 minutes and contain a number of questions to be solved. Teams of 1-3 will compete. Each team will be allowed a single laptop to share during the competition.
